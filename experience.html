Lenel Systems International

My most recent co-op. I worked with Typescript and Lenel's own OnGuard monitoring and video alarm systems to help develop a web video client capable of grabbing footage from a recorder and displaying it within seconds. My time at Lenel also spurred my personal growth, giving me the courage to engage with my coworkers outside of the office.

Omega by Parlec

My first co-operative working experience. I worked on creating fixes and developing features for P7, Omega by Parlec's custom machine shop tool program. I learned about the View-Presenter Model and Modular Programming design patterns while working in an Agile development environment.

Four Directions Productions

During the Summers while I was attending high school, I worked for Four Directions Productions, the Oneida Indian Nation's video and animation production house. While there I was teamed up with the company's tech guru Ryan Rogers and learned about production pipelines, differential data backup, and UML architecture.

Bimbo Bakeries USA

In the Summer of 2016, I took a seasonal full-time job at the Bimbo Bakeries USA warehouse in Canastota, New York. I was a warehouse order picker, which meant that I picked, stored, and stacked bread products that were to be shipped to retailers the following day. It was certainly a difficult job, but I persevered and learned valuable lessons about hard work.
