About Me

I grew up in New York state with my brother Mark, just outside of a little town called Oneida. After taking pre-kindergarten at a public school my parents decided to enroll me in Manlius Pebble Hill, a private school that was a haven for great teachers. For the next 13 years, I learned respect, self-advocacy, and of my passion for computer software. Once I completed the 12th grade, I enrolled in the Game Design and Development program at the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT), which is where I now spend the majority of my time.

Currently I am taking classes to complete the Game Design & Development curriculum at RIT. I am enjoying using my free time to continue exploring programming (my original passion) and also assisting RIT's Anime Club. My Anime Club duties also include helping to run Tora-Con, the largest anime fan convention in Western New York.
